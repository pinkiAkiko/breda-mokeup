About BREDA

BREDA has been established to promote development of schemes on non-conventional energy sources. It has been nominated as nodal agency to carry out the remote village electrification program. BREDA is one of the premier organizations working in the field of renewable energy development and energy conservation, registered under the Society Registration Act 1860 in 1987.
